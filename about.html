About

I am a UX/UI designer based in Vienna. Analytical and creative, I enjoy learning and thrive in international settings. Adaptive by nature, I enjoy new challenges and personal growth. I strive to have a positive impact in everything I do and highly value cooperation to achieve the best result.

Through my academic background in Human Sciences and International Relations I have a strong foundation in both qualitative and quantitative research methods. Career Foundry’s training program taught me the key skills to succeed as a UI designer.

I am bilingual in English and French and can speak German, Japanese and Spanish. Living in France, Japan, the Netherlands and now Austria has taught me how to be sensitive to different cultures and worldviews, giving me a multitude of avenues to express my ideas and creativity.

I look forward to working on inspiring projects to create visually striking and ergonomic designs. Feel free to get in touch !
