About

  • I am a UX/UI designer based in Vienna. Analytical and creative, I enjoy learning and thrive in international settings. Adaptive by nature, I enjoy new challenges and personal growth. I strive to have a positive impact in everything I do and highly value cooperation to achieve the best result.
  • Through my academic background in Human Sciences and International Relations I have a strong foundation in both qualitative and quantitative research methods. Career Foundry’s training program taught me the key skills to succeed as a UI designer including the principles of interaction design as well as a solid understanding of design knowledge, user research and testing models.
  • Living in France, Japan, the Netherlands and now Austria reflects my love of learning as well as an insatiable curiosity for the world around me. I am bilingual in English and French and can speak German, Japanese and Spanish.
  • My international background has taught me how to be sensitive to different cultures and worldviews, giving me a multitude of avenues to express my ideas and creativity. My diverse background reflects a journey of self discovery and adaptation that has enriched my viewpoints and grounded me.

I look forward to working on inspiring projects to create visually striking and ergonomic designs. Feel free to get in touch !
